3D Design & Printing

Modeling an object, printing it, and learning from the physical result.

Assignment

The assignment was to model and 3D print something. The documentation should show both the design process and the printed result, including what changed between the CAD model and the final part.

Model

This section is ready for the CAD screenshots, design constraints, dimensions, and notes on why the object needed to be 3D printed rather than cut from flat material.

Print

Useful details to add here include printer settings, filament type, orientation, support material, print time, and any cleanup after printing.

Reflection

Add what surprised you about the print: tolerance, surface finish, support removal, strength, or anything you would change in the next iteration.
